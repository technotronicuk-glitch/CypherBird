02_Data_Fragmentation_Protocol

SHARD

Your files are broken into encrypted fragments (shards) before they even leave your hardware.

SCATTER

Each shard is sent to a different physical server node across our global Tier-4 network.

SHIELD

Even if a node is breached, the attacker only finds a meaningless piece of encrypted noise.
