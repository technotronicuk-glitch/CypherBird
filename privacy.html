Privacy Protocol

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Zero-Log Policy

Cypherbird does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Our systems are built to be "blind" by design. We do not track IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or session metadata.

2. Encryption Standards

All communications within the Cypherbird nest are encrypted using AES-256 and RSA-4096 standards. Decryption keys stay on your hardware. If you lose your keys, we cannot recover your data.

3. Third-Party Interactions

We do not use third-party trackers, cookies, or analytics scripts (like Google Analytics). Our "AI Assistant" is a localized instance that does not feed data back to a central model training set.

The Sovereignty Pledge

Cypherbird exists to protect the individual from the collective. We will never comply with data requests that compromise our core zero-knowledge architecture, as we simply do not have the data to give.
