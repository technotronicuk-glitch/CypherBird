Operational Capabilities

Modular security for the modern ghost.

1. Zero-Cloud Secure Vault

An encrypted directory within your device hardware. Uses expo-secure-store to ensure that even if the phone is unlocked, the vault remains a black box without your 4-digit hardware salt.

2. Asymmetric Payload Encryption

Convert any image or text string into a Cypher-Text block. This payload can be sent via SMS, Email, or even posted publicly—it remains a meaningless string of characters to anyone without your specific identity hash.

3. Ephemeral Secure Chat

Direct peer-to-peer logic. No message history is stored in a database. When the session ends, the keys are purged from the device memory.
