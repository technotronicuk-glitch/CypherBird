Ultra Protocol: Large Asset Scrambling

The core of CypherBird Ultra. We solved the "Mobile Memory" bottleneck using proprietary Stream Processing.

  • Temporal Chunking: Large 4K videos and audio files are sliced into small, manageable binary segments in real-time.
  • Parallel Scrambling: Each chunk is encrypted independently, ensuring the phone's CPU never overheats or crashes during 1GB+ file processing.
  • Zero-Buffer Policy: No unencrypted data is ever written to temporary storage. It moves straight from the "Chunker" to the "Engine."

The Power of $2^{256}$

Every piece of data in CypherBird is locked behind an AES-256 bit wall. To put this in perspective:

  • Combinations: There are approximately $1.15 \times 10^{77}$ possible keys.
  • The "Heat Death" Factor: Even if you turned every atom in the observable universe into a supercomputer, it would take billions of years to brute-force a single vault.
  • Hardware Entropy: Your PIN is hashed using a salt unique to your phone's processor ID, making "Cloning" the app impossible.

FileSystem Isolation

CypherBird treats your images as "Binary Blobs," invisible to the OS gallery.

  • EXIF Stripping: All metadata (GPS coordinates, timestamps, device model) is permanently purged upon entry.
  • Sandbox Architecture: Using expo-file-system, we create a hidden directory. Other apps see it as "Empty Space," protecting you from cross-app data snooping.

Zero-Database Architecture

Traditional accounts are vulnerabilities. CypherBird uses local-only persistence.

  • Hardware Enclave: Security keys exist only in the **SecureStore**, the specialized security chip on your iPhone or Android.
  • Account-less Login: No email, no password, no server-side "reset" buttons. If you don't have the PIN, the data simply doesn't exist.
