Understanding the Sovereign Engine and your digital rights.

CypherBird is a Zero-Knowledge system. We do not store your keys, PINs, or recovery phrases on our servers. This ensures total privacy, but it means if you lose your credentials, we cannot recover your data. Your information is locked by you, for you.

We use a Client-Side Integrity Algorithm. Before encryption, your device locally checks for prohibited material (illegal or underage content). If a violation is detected, the engine will refuse to encrypt the file. We do not see your data, but we do not protect illegal acts.

Privacy is not a commodity, but maintaining a secure, unhackable infrastructure has costs. Your subscription funds server maintenance, constant security hardening, and ensures that you are the customer, not the product.
